Which colors should I choose to design my website?

Color is a massive aspect of branding. Colors can trigger certain feelings on sight. There is a lot behind color physcology.

Warm Colors such as Red, Orange and Yellow mostly remind us of the fire and sun. They are the best colors to use in your website when your goal is to drive attention , enthusiasm and motivation.

On the other hand, Cool Colors such as Blue, Purple and Green often provide a sense of calm and relaxation.

Neutral Colors mainly Gray, Black and White are used to tone down primary colors and provide balance in web design.

Cool and Neutral colors are great for business companies, and websites containing spacious information which can't be read on the run and should be comprehended in comfortable restful atmosphere.

The best way to choose colors is

  1. Choose a appropriate color scheme for your website triggering a desired response. (Don't use your favourite colors, understand the importance of evaluating a color scheme based on the brand and product of services being promoted.)
